Chair Cover Rentals
Since every event is a special event, our elegant chair covers are laundered and individually steamed to eliminate wrinkles and stains after each party. We then bag, seal and protect our chair covers so they are ready for the next event. Since Special Event Linens personally handles the delivery to your event site, extra chair covers and bows are brought in case there are any last minute additions to your party. Our chair covers are delivered on a hanger to your event to prevent any wrinkles and are delivered on time.
