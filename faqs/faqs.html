Frequently Asked Questions
How can chair covers help reduce the cost of my event?

Special Event Linens eliminates your need to order additional decorations such as full-length tablecloths, expensive overlays and elaborate floral centerpieces. The full-length linen look is achieved when chair covers are placed on all the chairs around a table. Chair covers also create a complete and sophisticated appearance making other decorations unnecessary.
Do I have to worry about shipping charges for chair covers?

Special Event Linens delivers chair covers to cities throughout the Greater Los Angeles area. We cater to the Southern California region so we can help eliminate other costly charges such as shipping expenses to and from your event location, insurance charges, deposits and other fees.
Do I have to put the chair covers on myself?

Special Event Linens is a full service rental company that professionally installs chair covers and ties bows individually around each chair so you do not have to involve your guests for setup or removal.
How do you protect your chair covers from wrinkles during delivery?

Special Event Linens personally handles the delivery to your event site. Our chair covers are brought to your location on hangars on a rolling rack. We do not deliver chair covers to you wrinkled in a box, which would be the case if you ordered chair covers from out of state.
Will your chair covers fit the chairs at my event location?

Special Event Linens offers different size chair covers to fit the most popular chair types. Please call us with your event location so we can deliver the correct size chair covers.
What if my need for chair covers increases at the last minute?

Special Event Linens always brings extra chair covers and bows in case there are any last minute additions to your party.
Do I have to clean up & box the chair covers after the event?

Special Event Linens delivers chair covers to your event site and also picks up from the location after your party. This eliminates your inconvenience of gathering the chair covers at the end of the event, boxing them up and shipping them back.
Are your chair covers laundered after each event?

Special Event Linens launders and steams the chair covers after every event to eliminate stains and wrinkles.
