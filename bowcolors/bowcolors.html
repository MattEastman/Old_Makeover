Bow Colors
Change the look of your next event with a variety of bow colors to match any theme! Our bows are made from a beautiful organza material approximately 10" inches wide & 3 yards long, designed to tie a perfect bow. In addition to using bows with our chair covers, many brides save money by ordering bows for use as table runners eliminating the need for expensive overlays.
